Declan Notes

Leaned Methology:

    1- The q tag adds a quotation to your website.

    2- The em tag emphasizes the text within the tag.

    3- The abbr tag abbreviates the text included in the tag, hovering over the text reveals the full phrase.

    4- The br tag creates a newline break in your website, this is very important in terms of creating space to organize your website.

    5- The sup tag superscripts any given text, this can be important for notation of growth rate and commercial identifiers.
