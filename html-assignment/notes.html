Declan Notes

Learned Methodology:

  • The q tag adds a quotation to your website.
  • The em tag emphasizes the text within the tag.
  • The abbr tag abbreviates the text included in the tag; hovering over the text reveals the full phrase.
  • The
    tag creates a newline break in your website, which is important for creating space and organizing content.
  • The sup tag superscripts any given text, which is useful for growth rates or commercial identifiers.
